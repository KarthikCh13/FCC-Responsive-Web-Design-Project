Anthony Stark (The Invincible Iron Man)

Anthony Edward "Tony" Stark was a billionaire industrialist, a founding member of the Avengers, and the former CEO of Stark Industries. A brash but brilliant inventor, Stark was self-described as a genius, billionaire, playboy, and philanthropist. With his great wealth and exceptional technical knowledge, Stark was one of the world's most powerful men following the deaths of his parents and enjoyed the playboy lifestyle for many years. Fighting many threats since his kidnapping and eventual escape from the Ten Rings terrorist organization, he with his superior intelligence and knack for developing advanced technologies full body combat suits, The Iron Man, he kept the world at bay from certain doom and helped progress and technoligical advacements. Ultimately sacrificing himself to protect Earth from the Man Titan, Thanos who successed in wiping out half the life in the universe. Him and the rest of the avengers were able to bring back all the life that was wiped out with the Time Travel technology built by stark.
